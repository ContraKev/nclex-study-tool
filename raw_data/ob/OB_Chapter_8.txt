=== SLIDE 7 ===
Bishop Score
What is the Bishop score?
It is a tool created to assess the status of the cervix and helps to determine how it is likely to respond to an induction.  The presence of increased fetal fibronectin at the cervix and a Bishop score above 6 determine cervical readiness for labor induction.  

Different measures are scored: dilation of cervix, consistency of cervix (firm, medium, soft), length of cervix, cervical effacement (%), position of cervix, station of presenting part related to ischial spines

Amnioinfusion
An amnioinfusion is the injection of warmed sterile saline or LR into the uterus via and IUPC during labor after the membranes have ruptured. 
Indications: 
Oligohydramnios
Umbilical cord compression
Goal of reducing recurrent variable decelerations in the fetal heart rate
Goal of diluting meconium-stained amniotic fluid to prevent meconium aspiration syndrome

=== SLIDE 66 ===
You make a protein called fetal fibronectin (fFN) during pregnancy. It helps keep the amniotic sac attached to your uterine lining during pregnancy. A fetal fibronectin test checks a person’s risk of preterm birth by measuring fFN in vaginal fluid.

=== SLIDE 67 ===
Signs of Impending Preterm Labor
A shortened cervix on ultrasound at 20 weeks may be predictive of preterm labor.
Diagnosis of preterm labor is based on cervical effacement and dilation of more than 2 cm.
A fibronectin test
Fibronectin is a protein produced by the fetal membranes and leaks into vaginal secretions if uterine activity, infection, or cervical effacement occurs.
The presence of fibronectin in vaginal secretions between 22 and 24 weeks’ gestation is predictive of preterm labor.

=== SLIDE 85 ===
Prolapsed Cord
Prolapsed cord occurs when the water or amniotic sac is broken and the cord slips between the head (which usually blocks the outlet being first) and baby and is apparent in the vagina or externally.
Immediate concern is circulation to fetus, baby is oxygenating only as long as cord is functioning correctly.
Notify Providers and staff for help, get women in Knee Chest, leave hand in to lift head from compressing cord further
May administer tocolytics like Terbutaline
May have to ride on stretcher into OR and stay in place during C/Section until cord is cut (45 minutes)

=== SLIDE 89 ===
Key points
Induction of labor is intentional initiation of labor before it occurs naturally.
Augmentation of labor is the stimulation of contractions after they started naturally.
A Bishop score above 6 may predict successful labor induction.
Nurse observes amniotic fluid characteristics, fetal condition, and contraction pattern.
Dystocia is the term used to describe a difficult labor, which can be caused by abnormality of the power, passenger, passage, or psyche of woman

=== SLIDE 90 ===
Key points continued
Nursing measures includes: encouraging position changes, aids to relaxation, and reminding the patient to void can promote a more normal labor. 
Nursing care after episiotomy or perineal lacerations includes comfort measures such as cold applications, analgesics, and wound assessment.
Infection is the most common hazard after membranes rupture prematurely, especially if there is a long interval before delivery.
Induction of labor is more effective if cervical ripening is achieved before oxytocin is administered to stimulate contractions.
Anaphylactoid syndrome occurs when amniotic fluid enters the woman’s circulation.
