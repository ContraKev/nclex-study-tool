Screening for Signs of Autism Preliminary symptoms may include No pointing, gesturing (e.g., bye-bye) by 12 months  No single words by 16 months No spontaneous two-word phrases by 24 months Loss of achieved language or social skills Important to rule out lead poisoning, hearing deficit, neurological disorders, musculoskeletal diseases, and inborn errors of metabolism. Copyright © 2019, Elsevier Inc. All rights reserved.
