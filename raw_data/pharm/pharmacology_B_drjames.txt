 Food drug interactions  
Food  Effect  
Food with high Vit K  
(green leafy vegetables such as spinach, kale, 
broccoli, and Brussels sprouts ) • ↓ effect of warfarin = ↑ risk blood clot  
Food rich in potassium  
(banana, leafy green vegetables, avodacos, 
potatoes, watermelon, cantaloupe, beets, 
guava, coconut water, milk)  • Watch out for hyperkalemia in patient taking 
potassium -sparing diuretics, ACE -I, ARBs.  
Calcium -rich foods and supplements  
(e.g., yogurt, milk)  • Affects absorption of tetracyclines, fluoroquinolones.  
• ↓ effect of levothyroxine  
Grapefruit juice  • ↑ effect of warfarin  
• ↑ effect of statin  
• ↑ effect of calcium channel blockers  
Tyramine rich food  
(aged cheese, cured meat, hot dogs, bacon, 
processed food, soybean products, alcohol)  • Tyramine syndrome (hypertension, tachycardia, HA, 
N/V) with MAO -I (phenelzine, selegiline , rasagilin e)  
Alcohol  • ↑ effect of warfarin  
• Avoid concurrent use with CNS depressants  
• Avoid meds with possible hepatotoxicity  
• Disulfiram reaction (hypotension, palpitations, 
flushing, sweating, N/V) with metronidazole, some 
cephalosporins, sulfonylureas (glyburide, glipizide)
