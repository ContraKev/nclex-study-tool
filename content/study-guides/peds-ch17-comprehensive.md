## 📊 Toddler Development Quick Reference

### Physical Development

| Milestone | Age |
|-----------|-----|
| Birthweight quadruples | 2.5 years |
| Deciduous teeth complete | 2.5 years |
| Bowel/bladder control | 2.5-3 years |
| Myelination of spinal cord complete | ~2 years |

### Erikson: Autonomy vs Shame and Doubt

- **Age:** 1-3 years
- **Crisis:** Autonomy vs Shame/Doubt
- **Key task:** Independence, toilet training
- **Parent role:** Offer limited choices, maintain safety, positive self-image
- **Negativism:** Counter with limited choices and distraction
- **Ritualism:** Compulsive routines provide security

### Piaget: Sensorimotor → Preconceptual

- Sensory and motor functions synchronize
- Memory strengthens
- Trial, error, repetition learning
