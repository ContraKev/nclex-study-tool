# PEDS Exam 1 Cheatsheet — Ch15-19

**Topics:** The Family, Infancy & Childhood, Adolescence, Hospitalized Child, Variations

## FAMILY
- Family types: nuclear, blended, extended, single-parent, foster
- FCC: family is constant/expert, dignity & respect, information sharing, participation, collaboration
- Parenting styles: authoritarian, authoritative, permissive, uninvolved
- Cultural: silence before answering = thinking (not shyness); respect hot/cold balance beliefs
- Family Apgar: Adaptation, Partnership, Growth, Affection, Resolve

## GROWTH & DEVELOPMENT PRINCIPLES
- Cephalocaudal: head→toe (lifts head before sits)
- Proximodistal: center→periphery (whole hand before pincer)
- Growth: measurable (height, weight, HC). Weight doubles 6mo, triples 12mo
- Development: mastery of skills; general→specific

## ERIKSON (Psychosocial)
| Age | Stage | Key Task |
|-----|-------|----------|
| 0-1 | Trust vs Mistrust | Consistent care → hope |
| 1-3 | Autonomy vs Shame | Independence, choices → self-control |
| 3-6 | Initiative vs Guilt | Play, fantasy → purpose |
| 6-12 | Industry vs Inferiority | School tasks → competence |
| 12-18 | Identity vs Role Confusion | "Who am I?" → fidelity |

## PIAGET (Cognitive)
| Stage | Age | Key |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Sensorimotor | 0-2 | Object permanence ~8-9mo |
| Preoperational | 2-7 | Egocentric, magical thinking, centration |
| Concrete Operational | 7-11 | Conservation, classification, logic |
| Formal Operational | 11+ | Abstract, hypothetical |

## KOHLBERG (Moral)
- Preconventional (<9): punishment/reward
- Conventional (9-13): social approval, rules
- Postconventional (13+): justice, ethics

## INFANT (0-12mo)
- Milestones: social smile 1-3mo, rolls 4-6mo, sits 6-9mo, pincer 9-12mo, walks assist 12mo
- Nutrition: breast/formula only 0-6mo; solids 6mo (iron cereal first); no honey <1yr; no cow milk <1yr
- Safety: back to sleep, rear-facing car seat, no small objects, water heater ≤120°F
- Red flags: 3mo can't lift head prone; 10mo doesn't sit steadily

## TODDLER (1-3yr)
- Milestones: walks 12-15mo, 2-3 word sentences 24mo, runs/jumps 24mo, pedal tricycle 36mo
- Nutrition: food jags normal; limit juice 4-6oz/day; milk 16-24oz max
- Safety: rear-facing until age 2; poison locks; drowning supervision; burns prevention
- Play: parallel play; push/pull toys

## PRESCHOOL (3-6yr)
- Milestones: full sentences, cooperative play, draws person 3+ parts, imaginary friends
- Safety: bike helmet, stranger danger, screen time ≤2hr, dental
- Play: cooperative/fantasy play

## SCHOOL-AGE (6-12yr)
- Milestones: reads/writes, concrete operational, peer relationships, industry
- Safety: helmet, seatbelt, internet safety, 60min exercise/day
- Play: competitive, rule-based

## ADOLESCENCE (12-18yr)
- Erikson: Identity vs Role Confusion
- HEADSS: Home, Education/Employment, Activities, Drugs, Sexuality, Suicide/Depression
- Vaccines: Tdap, HPV, MenACWY
- Nutrition: increased calcium/iron/zinc
- Risks: MVA #1 death, depression, eating disorders

## HOSPITALIZED CHILD
- Regression: normal, temporary. "Toileting skills return when feeling better"
- Separation anxiety stages: Protest → Despair → Detachment
- Preparation by age:
  - Infant: no prep (react to parent)
  - Toddler: just before, simple words
  - Preschool: day before, play with equipment
  - School-age: day before, thorough explanation
  - Adolescent: written info, involve in decisions
- Atraumatic care: minimize separation, prevent pain, promote control, therapeutic play
- FCC: encourage parent stay, maintain routines, involve in care

## VARIATIONS
- Increased ICP infant: bulging fontanel, high-pitched cry, irritability
- Spina bifida: latex precautions (allergy risk)
- DDH: Ortolani/Barlow, asymmetric thigh folds; Pavlik harness
- Coarctation: decreased BP/pulses in legs
- Sickle cell: hydration, pain management; avoid ice
- Hemophilia: factor VIII, ice/elevation; avoid NSAIDs
- Post-tonsillectomy: frequent swallowing = hemorrhage
- Digoxin: hold if HR <90 (infant), <70 (child); toxicity = vomiting, bradycardia

## PAIN ASSESSMENT
- FLACC: infants/nonverbal
- FACES: 3+ years
- Numeric: 7+ years

## SAFETY BY AGE
| Age | Key Concerns |
|-----|--------------|
| Infant | SIDS, car seat, choking, burns |
| Toddler | Poisoning, drowning, falls, burns |
| Preschool | Bike, stranger danger, screen time |
| School-age | Helmet, seatbelt, internet, sports |
| Adolescent | MVA, substance use, depression |

## HIGH-YIELD
- Weight: doubles 6mo, triples 12mo
- Teeth: 20 baby teeth; 6 by 1yr; complete by 2.5yr
- Play: parallel (toddler), cooperative (preschool), competitive (school-age)
- Hospitalization: regression normal, encourage parent presence
- FCC: family is expert, involve in all decisions
- Atraumatic care: minimize pain/separation, promote control
- Leading death >1yr: unintentional injury
- Red flags: can't lift head 3mo, can't sit 10mo
- Immunizations: HepB at birth, DTaP/IPV/Hib/Rotavirus at 2mo, MMR/Varicella at 12mo
