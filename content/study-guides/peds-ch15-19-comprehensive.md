### Erikson Psychosocial Stages

| Stage | Age | Crisis | Key Task |
|-------|-----|--------|----------|
| Trust vs Mistrust | 0-1 yr | Trust | Consistent care builds trust |
| Autonomy vs Shame/Doubt | 1-3 yr | Autonomy | Independence, toilet training |
| Initiative vs Guilt | 3-5 yr | Initiative | Purpose through play |
| Industry vs Inferiority | 6-12 yr | Industry | Competence, school mastery |

### Piaget Cognitive Stages

| Stage | Age | Key Feature |
|-------|-----|-------------|
| Sensorimotor | 0-2 yr | Object permanence, trial & error |
| Preoperational | 2-7 yr | Magical thinking, egocentrism |
| Concrete Operational | 7-11 yr | Logical thinking, conservation |
